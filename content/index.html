Posting Tips

Best times to post

  • Google Business Profile: Mon-Fri 8-11 AM. Post weekly minimum to keep your profile active.
  • Facebook: Tue-Thu 9 AM-12 PM, or Sun 7-9 PM for local audiences.
  • Instagram: Tue/Wed/Fri 10 AM-1 PM. Add a real photo or a 5-sec before/after Reel for 3x reach.
  • Blog: Tue or Wed mornings. Update the page date when you refresh content for SEO freshness.

What to add to every post

  • A real photo from a recent job (before/after gets the most engagement).
  • The neighborhood or city name when possible — locals notice.
  • A clear next step: call, message, or "Get a free quote at splashwashing.com".

SEO + AEO quick rules

  • SEO: Use the service + city in the H1, first paragraph, and one image alt tag. Don't repeat — read it out loud, if it sounds stuffed, it is.
  • AEO: Include a question as a header (e.g. "How often should I pressure wash my house?") with a 40-60 word answer right under it. That's what voice search and AI overviews pull.
  • Internal-link every blog post to your nearest service page.
